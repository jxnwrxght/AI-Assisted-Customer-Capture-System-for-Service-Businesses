# Contractor Customer Acquisition & Retention System

An AI-assisted customer lifecycle management system designed to help service businesses capture leads, organize customer information, improve follow-up, generate repeat business, and increase customer retention.

---

## Current Status

Pilot implementation and system design.

Core workflows have been designed and partially built, including:

* Missed call response automation
* Missed text response automation
* CRM integration
* Customer intake workflows
* Review request workflows
* Maintenance reminder workflows

The next phase is deployment with a live contractor partner to evaluate operational impact and identify opportunities for further improvement.

---

## Background

While speaking with local contractors that I work with in my real estate business, I noticed a common pattern: most were not struggling to perform the work itself. They were struggling with the systems surrounding the work.

Customer information was often spread across personal phones, text messages, handwritten notes, and memory. Missed calls and texts could easily become missed opportunities. Follow-up processes were inconsistent, maintenance reminders rarely happened, and previous customers often disappeared after the initial job was completed.

The challenge wasn't just generating more leads - the challenge was creating a repeatable system to capture, organize, and retain the customers they already had.

This project explores how AI-assisted workflows and automation can support customer acquisition, customer retention, and business operations while keeping contractors in control of customer relationships.

## Operational Challenges

Many service businesses rely heavily on the owner's phone as the primary operating system for the company.

This creates several operational challenges:

* Missed calls become missed opportunities
* Missed texts go unanswered
* Customer information becomes difficult to organize
* Follow-up activity is inconsistent
* Maintenance opportunities are forgotten
* Review requests are rarely automated
* Previous customers are not systematically re-engaged

As the business grows, these inefficiencies compound and become increasingly difficult to manage manually.

## Lessons Learned

One of the biggest insights from early contractor conversations was that the problem often isn't lead generation.

The larger issue is customer organization, follow-up consistency, and creating systems that continue generating value after the initial job is complete.

Improving customer lifecycle management may create more value than simply increasing lead volume.
